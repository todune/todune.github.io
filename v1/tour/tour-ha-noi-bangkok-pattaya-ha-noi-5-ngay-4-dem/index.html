TOUR HÀ NỘI – BANGKOK – PATTAYA – HÀ NỘI 5 NGÀY 4 ĐÊM

Highlights of the journey

Thailand - A country located in Southeast Asia, famous for its golden temples and majestic, magnificent palaces, friendly, hospitable and open-minded people. Coming to Thailand, you will enjoy the fresh air of the sea, the sacredness of the temples, the noise and bustle of the bars and the friendliness of the people here. In particular, you can enjoy the unique "Show" performances.

 

Highlights:

  • Free meals at the Baiyoke Sky building (the tallest 86-storey building in Bangkok)
  • Visit the four-region floating marketPattaya (Pattaya Floating Market)
  • Visit the largest Siracha Tiger Zoo park in Thailand Lan
  • Take a speedboat and swim in the clear blue water at Coral Island - Coral Island
  • Visit the jewelry center, gold, silver, and gemstones, cosmetics supermarket World Gem Gallery
  • Enjoy the most spectacular Bede show in Thailand at the stage simulating the arena Roma Italia
  • Visit and shop at the 3-region specialty center Saithip Garden
  • Shop to your heart's content at the supermarket specializing in the most unique leather goods
  • Cruise on the mother river to see the city of Bangkok on both banks
  • Worship the sacred four-faced Buddha at Erawan Bangkok Shrine
  • Worship the sacred Buddha Relic Temple Wat Yannawa
  • Visit the national treasure of Thailand Wat Traimit
  • Enjoy shopping at shopping malls Patunam,Platium wholesale market, Central World, Big C…

05h30’: Car and tour guide pick you up at the meeting point. Depart for terminal T2 Noi Bai International Airport, you check in for flight (VN611 at 08:50' - 10:50')

DAY 2 | PATTAYA - CORAL ISLAND – WORLD GEM GALLERY – BILLIONAIRE PALACE – KHAO CHEECHAN – COLOSSUEM SHOW (BREAKFAST / LUNCH / DINNER)

Morning: You will have breakfast at the hotel. Then the car takes the group to Pattaya beach, you board the speedboat Coral Island. You can participate in games such as: parasailing, fishing, surfing, driving a scooter on the sea, exploring the seabed... (self-paid). You are free to swim in Pattaya beach with clear blue water, long white sand beach. Return to the mainland, you have lunch at the restaurant.

Afternoon: The car and tour guide take the group to visit:

– The World Gems Gallery Gold and Silver Processing Center is the largest and most famous in Thailand. It displays works of art made from gemstones by Thai artisans: statues of peacocks, tigers, and dragons made entirely of gold and gemstones; gemstone mosaics: eight horses, eight immortals crossing the sea... In addition, this is also the place where gemstone trading sessions with ISO 9002 certification on quality management take place, where famous beautiful gemstones are displayed, providing useful information about the process of mining and processing gemstones in Thailand and around the world.

– Billionaire's Castle Baan Sukhawadee– Located by the sea, the main architecture is inspired by the palaces and palaces of Versailles, offering an architecturally appealing view. The castle also contains hidden meanings about philosophy of life and profound religion.

– Khao CheeChan Buddha Mountain – The mountain has a golden statue carved by laser on the cliff, using 999kg of gold, 130m high. Surrounded by a variety of flowers in meticulously maintained gardens. The area is protected by the Royal Thai Navy.

The car takes the group to dinner at the restaurant, then takes the group to enjoy the show Show Colloseumonly in Thailand – Professional, modern art program, with the colors of many countries performed by transgender actors. The group returns to the hotel to rest and freely explore the city at night.

Stay overnight at MANTRA PURA RESORT hotel or equivalent.

DAY 3 | PTY – FLOATING MARKET – SAITHIP GARDEN - POISONOUS SNAKE RESEARCH CENTER – LEATHER SHOP (BREAKFAST/LUNCH/DINNER)

Morning: After breakfast at the hotel, you check out. The car takes the Departure group back to Bangkok. On the way, visit:

– Four Regions Floating Market (Pattaya Floating Market) – This is the only floating market that combines many different Thai architectural styles, a perfect example of the integration of commercialization and art.

– Saithip Garden (Saithip Garden) – Is a place to display products from the three regions of the Kingdom of Thailand. You can freely shop for specialties such as: Canned Blood Bird's Nest, Natural Honey, Tiger Bone Glue, ...).

– Royal Snake Research Center: You will enjoy the snake catching show with bare hands, then you will listen to pharmacists introduce and explain the uses of rare snakes in the kingdom of Thailand and the medicines made from snakes that Thai people have researched for more than 100 years.

– Leather Factory Outlets is a famous store in Thailand. You can learn about the processing and classification of animal leather products, shopping at the Royal Leather Center.

You will have lunch at the restaurant, then continue to move to Bangkok city.

You will have dinner at the restaurant with special dishes - Hotpot Japanese Suki.

Stay overnight at the S.D AVENUE BANGKOK hotel or equivalent.

DAY 4 | WAT TRAIMIT - ERAWWAN – SHOPPING (BREAKFAST, LUNCH,_)

Morning: Have breakfast at the hotel. The car will take you to visit:

– Wat Traimit Golden Buddha Temple - One of the most sacred temples in Thailand with the Golden Buddha statue Golden Buhhdha located in the main hall – The largest Golden Buddha statue in the world: 3 meters high and weighing more than 5.5 tons. The statue was cast in the quiet Sukhothai style and was discovered by chance in the 1950s. Locals believe that this largest statue in the world represents prosperity and purity as well as strength and power.

You will have lunch at Baiyoke Sky building (the tallest 86-storey building in Bangkok) and enjoy over 500 special dishes, a combination of culinary cultures of both Europe and Southeast Asia.

Afternoon: The car will take you to go shopping at major shopping centers:

+ Big C: 4-storey shopping mall located on Ratchada Road, From here, you can visit Erawan Four-Faced Buddha Temple - Located right in the center of Erawan Bangkok, is one of the sacred temples in Thailand, where people and tourists from all over come to visit and offer incense to pray for luck, happiness, and prosperity.

+ Central World: Is the largest shopping mall in Thailand, and one of the largest shopping malls in the world.

+ Platunam Market : Is a market located at the intersection of Ratchaprop and Petchburi, specializing in providing items such as clothes, shoes, fashion accessories at wholesale prices.

Evening: You pay for your own dinner coupon at the supermarket.

Stay overnight at S.D AVENUE BANGKOK hotel or equivalent.

DAY 5 | WATYANAWWA – HANOI (BREAKFAST, LUNCH,_,)

Morning: Guests have breakfast at the hotel and check out. The group continues the journey to:

– Watyanawa Temple also known as Relic Temple – One of the most sacred temples in Thailand, with the temple building designed in the shape of a boat along with the Thai architecture of steep roofs, reflecting the style of the Ayutthaya period. It is located right next to the mother river, the Chao Phraya. This temple is known for preserving many relics of enlightened monks. Devotees come here to seek a relic as a symbol of peace and to express their sincere devotion.

The bus will take guests to the pier to board the cruise on the Chao Phraya River – the mother river of Thailand. Tourists can enjoy the scenery along the riverbanks and observe the phenomenon of fish rising on the large river.

Guests will have lunch at the airport.

After that, guests will continue shopping until it's time to head to the airport for the check-in procedure for flight (VN614 15:55 – 17:55) back to Hanoi.

18:00: Arrive at Noi Bai Airport, the bus will take the group back to the original pick-up point, and we bid farewell, hoping to see you again on future journeys!
